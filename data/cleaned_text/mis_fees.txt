4131. Miscellaneous Fees 
1.1. Below is the list and rates of miscellaneous fees: 
1.1.1. Admission Application Fee 
40 AZN 
1.1.2. Transcript Fee 
10 AZN per transcript 
1.1.3. Parking Fee 
750 AZN per year in Building B. 
Monthly fees are not available 
1.1.4. Printing Fee 
as determined by outside vendor 
1.2. The University charges some fines and penalties due to lateness, misdemeanor, 
and/or reckless behavior of students. Below is the list and rates of such fines and 
penalties: 
 
1.2.1. Late Return of Books 
50 AZN per book 
1.2.2. Loss of or Damage to Book 
25 AZN flat administrative charge, 
plus cost of recovery 
1.2.3. Loss of or Damage to Student ID 
card 
30 AZN 
1.2.4. Parking or Traffic Violation 
50 AZN per occurrence 
1.2.5. Damage to Property/Facility 
250 AZN flat fee, plus cost of 
recovery 
1.2.6. Penalty for Smoking Beyond 
Designated Areas 
250 AZN flat fee is charged for 
each occurrence 
1.3. All fines and penalties must be paid in 10 calendar days. Beyond this grace period, 
late payment fee and interest will be charged.
