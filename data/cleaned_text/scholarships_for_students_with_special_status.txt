4251. Students with A Status of Internally Displaced Person, Children of 
Disabled War Veterans, Children Deprived of Parental Care, Limited 
Health ability, and Children from Family of Martyrs 
1.1. Incoming students with a status of internally displaced person (IDP), children of 
disabled war veterans (CDWV), war veterans (WVT), children deprived of parental 
care (CDPC), limited health ability (DLH), and children from family of martyrs 
(CFM) receive a 100 percent tuition waiver for the first year of study upon providing 
required documentation. 
1.2. Special status scholarships are valid for students who apply for the Bachelor dual 
degree programs subsidized by public funding. At the graduate level, special status 
scholarship does not apply to dual degree programs. 
1.3. Undergraduate students can study maximum 5 years (10 semesters) if they are enrolled 
to EAPP program or 4 years (8 semesters) if they are directly enrolled to a degree 
program by using Special Status Scholarship, whereas graduate students can study 
maximum 2 years (4 semesters) by using Special Status Scholarship. 
1.4. Special Status scholarship covers one education degree only one time for one level. 
1.5. The students lose their Special Status waive if they reapply to degree through the State 
Examination Center of the Republic of Azerbaijan and ADA Admission office.
