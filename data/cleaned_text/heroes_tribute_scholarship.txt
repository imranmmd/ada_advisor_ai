4261. Heroes Tribute Scholarship 
1.1. Incoming and returning students can apply to Heroes Tribute Scholarship during their 
tenure at ADA University. 
1.2. Incoming and returning student must meet one of the below requirements in order to 
be eligible for the Heroes Tribute Scholarship: 
1.2.1. Prospective student and/or enrolled student must be a current member of the 
Armed Services of the Republic of Azerbaijan 
1.2.2. Prospective student and/or enrolled student must be a “National Hero” or a 
child of a person with “National Hero” status 
1.2.3. Prospective student and/or enrolled student must be a child of a person who 
has been disabled or fallen as a result of 20 January events 
1.2.4. Prospective student and/or enrolled student must be a child or spouse of a 
military serviceman wounded in war 
1.2.5. Prospective student and/or enrolled student must be a spouse of a military 
serviceman fallen in war 
1.2.6. Prospective student and/or enrolled student must be a child of a current military 
serviceman and/or Armed Forces pensioner with military disability. 
1.3. Eligible students receive a 100 percent tuition waiver for the first semester of study. 
1.4. As of the second semester, returning students who accumulate a Grade Point Average 
(GPA) of 2.00 or higher or successfully pass EAPP level in undergraduate and 2.75 or 
higher in graduate programs receive a 100 percent tuition waiver. These standards are 
for the semester. 
1.5. Eligible students must maintain a full load coursework by earned hours. Students on 
academic probation are required to take one course fewer than a full course load in order 
to maintain their scholarship. 
1.6. Undergraduate students can study maximum 4 years/8 terms in a degree program (Fall 
and Spring terms only) plus maximum 1 year/2 terms in an EAPP program starting 
from the admission term by using Heroes Tribute Scholarship, whereas graduate 
students can study maximum 2 years/4 terms (Fall and Spring terms only) starting 
from the admission term by using Heroes Tribute Scholarship. 
1.7. In case of prolongation of the study for documented medical emergencies and/or other 
personal issues out of students’ control, Heroes Tribute Scholarship may be awarded 
for one additional term only.
=== PAGE 3 ===
1.8. The summer term fee is not covered by Heroes Tribute Scholarship.
