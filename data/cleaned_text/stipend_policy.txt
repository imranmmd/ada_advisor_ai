4232. Stipends for students with presidential scholarship 
 
Incoming and returning students will now receive presidential stipends only after confirmation by 
the Ministry of Science and Education: 
• 
Incoming students will receive a presidential stipend once the University receives the 
official nomination list from the Ministry of Science and Education. 
• 
Returning students become eligible for the continuation of stipend after the University 
submits the list of students with their academic information, and the list of recipients is 
confirmed by the Ministry of Science and Education. 
• 
Stipends are transferred only after official confirmation by the Ministry of Science and 
Education. 
The requirements for the presidential scholarship: 
2. Students admitted as presidential stipend recipients must meet all of the 
requirements below at the end of each term for the continuation of their stipend in 
the next term: 
a. Minimum term GPA of 3.67, which will be further converted to the Ministry of 
Science and Education grading system and should accumulate above 91 points. 
b. Minimum A as final grade for all courses taken in the semester. 
c. Students must have the full load by the attempted hours in the previous academic 
term. 
2.1 Students can receive this stipend for 4 years (8 semesters) only. Students are not eligible for 
the stipends for the period of study in the EAPP program (except for their first term at ADA 
University). 
2.2 Students do not receive this stipend for the term after their exchange semester abroad.
